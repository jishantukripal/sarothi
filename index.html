    Sarothi Organization

    A non-governmental old age home in Jorhat, Assam.

    Sarothi is one of the very first old age home in upper Assam located in the Southeastern outskirts of the city of Jorhat in a sub urban area with good road communication. It is produly creating impact by the following objectives :

    • To provide shelter to underprivileged and old women of society.
    • To help the underprivileged and old women.
    • To provide them healthcare, food, and most importantly love and care.

    The NGO is managed by a committee consisting of executive members. Please feel free to Contact Us regarding any queries or your assistance.
